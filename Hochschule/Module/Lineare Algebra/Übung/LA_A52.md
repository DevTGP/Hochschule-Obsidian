# $v_{1}$

$\begin{pmatrix}1 & 1 & 1 & | & 4 \\ 0 & 1 & 1 & | & 3 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 & | & 2\end{pmatrix}$
$\to \lambda_{3} = 2$

$\begin{pmatrix}1 & 1 & | & 2 \\ 0 & 1 & | & 1\end{pmatrix}$
$\to \lambda_{2} = 1$

$\begin{pmatrix}1 & | &  1\end{pmatrix}$
$\to \lambda_{1} = 1$

$v_{1} =\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ 1 \\ 2\end{pmatrix}_{B}$
